Programmes & Selection Process

Indian Institute Of Technology, Hyderabad

Undergraduate( B.TECH )
Duration: 4 years
Qualifying Test : IIT-JEE
Minor & Honours: Option to earn an Honours degree (in the same department) or a Minor degree (in another department.). This requires taking an additional five specially selected courses.

Graduate(M.Tech)
Duration: 2 years
Thesis duration: 1 year
Assistantship: Affiliated to course instructors or research projects.
Qualifying Test : GATE(Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering).

Graduate(M.Sc.)
Duration: 2 years
M.Sc. project duration: 1 year.
Qualifying Test : JAM(Joint Admission Test For MSc).

Doctoral Programme
The PhD programme gives an opportunity to enthusiastic researchers to take up open-ended research problems. Both computational as well as experimental works addressing engineering and scientific complexities and concepts are undertaken to meet the needs of the industry and academia alike.
Selection : Students with good academic background are admitted into the programme through a rigorous interview. Assistantship for regular PhD students is provided by MHRD.
