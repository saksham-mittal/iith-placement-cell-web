U. B. DESAI

Director, IIT Hyderabad

In July 2017 IIT Hyderabad entered its 10th year. We have come a long way in the last 9 years. Academically, we are at the forefront of developing new curricula and new programs. We are also at the forefront of research and development. Our faculty student ratio is the best among all IITs -- 1:13. We have a very strong PG program. The rough ratio among Ph.D. students, Masters students and undergraduate students is 30:25:45. Our placement records is also very good -- in most departments all students get placed, with highest salary around 35 lakhs per annum.
